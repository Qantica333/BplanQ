Qantica Business Plan V3b - May 2025

A comprehensive vision of Qantica: Mission, Technology, and Strategy

Executive Summary

We define Qantica as a technology company serving the creative process. We officially started Qantica in Q3 2024, but we have been understanding the nature of the business and seeking solutions since 2022.

Through conversations with prominent industry figures, we have perceived a crisis in the Intellectual Property (IP) and creative process universe: approximately 98% of movie projects fail, quality is decreasing, and there is evident "remote control fatigue" among audiences. This led us to realize that the problems were much larger than we initially thought and that technology's role could be revolutionary. That was the genesis of Qantica.

We have developed original technology that guides the creator without interfering with their creative essence, optimizing resources and time. The platform will be able to process scripts simultaneously and in a highly scalable way, depending on our global marketing strategy. Only IPs deemed complete and ready for streaming platforms or video games will be selected for distribution.

Creative Process Crisis and Solution

Problem Identified

98% of movie projects fail. The quality of works is decreasing, and there is evident "remote control fatigue" in the audience.

Our Value Proposition

Qantica guides the creator without interfering with their creative essence, optimizing resources and time. Our technology simultaneously and scalably processes scripts, selecting only completed IPs ready for distribution on streaming platforms or video games.

Investment Stages and Platform Vision

PROFILES

In this first layer, screenwriters' profiles are received and classified. Previously rejected, completed but novel, and in-progress IPs are accepted. Profiles are modeled in databases, with unique IDs and access permissions that will allow for NFT ID management. The goal is to reach 100,000 members.

EDEN - PLATFORM

This is the backoffice where works are curated, summaries are written, and digital content is generated. IPs are "stressed" to determine their full potential as films, games, or both. The proprietary QW3 process (US patent pending) is executed here. It operates as a "Dark Factory" where validation focuses on the outputs (generated multimedia files), while internal multimedia content creation processes are performed automatically by specialized AI agents. The mission is to produce 20,000 ready works.

DASHBOARD

The public-facing interface where material produced in the EDEN is presented. Through blockchain technology, interactive and playful elements are added. The platform aims for 100,000 active members to participate in IP curation and selection through voting, and also to participate in selective crowdfunding.

Core Technology

  • BLOCKCHAIN: Legitimacy, Traceability, Immutability, Security, Smart Contracts, Token Creation (one token per word, for example), NFTs (Dynamic, Creator, Fan), Crowdfunding.
  • SMART CONTRACTS: Integrated to automate processes.
  • TOKEN CREATION: Will facilitate a potential next financing round and will be a utility token.
  • NFTS: For creators and fans.
  • PLATFORM TOKENIZATION: The tokenization of the platform's IPs.

AI and Content Generation

  • AI Programmers: Creation of "masters," script analysis, and work improvement with a proprietary and patented process.
  • Automatic Generation: Creation of teasers and project budgets.
